Frequently asked questions

Common questions about Artist Services.

Do I need to be on Legendary to buy Artist Services?
No. Any package, any plan, any artist. A Build artist can buy Campaign Management. A Legendary artist can buy Audience Development. Artist Services are completely separate from your support tier and have nothing to do with which plan you're on.
I'm on Legendary. Do I get advisory sessions AND Artist Services?
Yes — they stack. Legendary gives you 4 x monthly advisory sessions focused on platform guidance. Artist Services is a separate engagement where a D2F specialist executes work for you. Different products, different purposes. Legendary artists can and should add Artist Services for campaigns and key moments.
How is Artist Services different from Platform Support?
Support answers "how do I do this in Openstage?" Artist Services answers "what should I be doing to grow, and can someone help build or run it?" Support is reactive. Artist Services is prescriptive, custom, and hands-on execution.
Who actually does the work?
A D2F specialist — not the support team. Your specialist is a separate engagement, scoped and signed off before work starts. They understand the artist, the fanbase, and the goals they're building toward.
How does billing work?
30-day cycles from date of purchase. One-off for single activations. Minimum 3 months for ongoing Campaign Management. Anything outside scope is billed against the rate card — that conversation happens before work expands, not after.
My campaign is longer than 30 days. What do I do?
Campaign Management is built for this. Multiple blocks are available for longer campaigns and multi-month arcs. The 3-month minimum for ongoing engagements is the starting point, not the ceiling. Talk to the team and we'll scope it correctly from the start.
What if I need more than what was scoped?
Anything outside the original scope is billed against the rate card. That conversation happens before work expands — never after. All scope changes are agreed in writing before they begin.
When will Artist Services be available?
Launching with a small pilot group first. Talk to the team to get on the list. We're not scaling delivery until the pilot is validated and onboarding resource is confirmed.
